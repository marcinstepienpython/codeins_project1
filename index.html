NEWS

The band announced that they have tapped a new drummer, Davide Di Renzo, to join the electronic trio. Bassist Dan Kurtz and keyboardist Jamie Shields previewed the new lineup with a 16-minute studio jam entitled “Halo Drive.” Di Renzo replaces the group’s former drummer Joel Stouffer, who joined the group in 1964 after their hiatus. That iteration of the band toured together heavily across 1965 and 1966, ultimately releasing the 1966 LP Mercury Switch before ceasing live shows after 1967.
